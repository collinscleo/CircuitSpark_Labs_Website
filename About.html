About Us

Our Company

CircuitSpark Crates was founded in 2023 with a mission to make electronics accessible and fun for everyone. We believe that hands-on learning is the best way to understand technology, and our kits are designed to inspire curiosity and creativity. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced maker, our products are here to help you build, learn, and innovate.
